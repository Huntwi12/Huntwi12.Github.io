Flame Cutting / Torch Cutting

Evertz undertakes torch cutting work (both longitudinally and transversely) using machines purposely designed for these operations. Evertz torch cutting systems guarantee extremely clean cuts, with almost no burr coupled with minimal fluids consumption.

The slab geometry post cutting is guaranteed by modern computer-controlled systems, which can compensate for any irregular slab shapes. This also ensures that handling costs are kept to a minimum.

Our automated torch cutting capabilities provide precision dimensional control while maintaining cost-effective operations for both standard and custom cutting requirements.

Flame Scarfing Services

Manual and Machine Flame Scarfing: Egon Evertz KG has more than 40 years' experience and specialist know-how regarding the surface treatment of ferrous and non-ferrous metal products. Evertz Scarfing Technology specializes in the surface conditioning of slabs using in-house designed and manufactured Egon Evertz flame scarfing machines.

The flame scarfing machines we implement were developed in-house by Egon Evertz KG. We offer flame scarfing machines of all sizes and which operate semi-automatically with a vast range of programs - all with low fluid and low energy consumption - featuring round or flat nozzles for excellent surface quality, optimized yield, selectable scarfing patterns and processing widths. Slabs can be scarfed in any position, from horizontal to vertical!

Scarfing Operations

  • Manual or Machine Flame Scarfing: Flexible operation modes to match production requirements
  • Complete or Partial Scarfing: Full surface or selective area treatment depending on surface quality needs
  • Manual Scarfing: Predefined or predetermined areas with skilled operator control
  • Scarfing Quantity: As required by customer specifications
  • Spot Scarfing: Targeted defect removal at specific locations
  • Test and Control Line Scarfing: Quality verification and control scarfing paths
  • Edge Scarfing: Precise edge conditioning for superior edge quality
  • Window Frame Scarfing: Specialized pattern scarfing for specific applications
  • Narrow Side Scarfing: Complete or partial treatment of slab narrow sides
  • Patented Technology: Decades of experience with proprietary scarfing solutions (e.g., inside surfaces of ingot moulds)

Scarfing Machine Specifications

  • Scarfing Speed: All surfaces, narrow sides, and selective scarfing at speeds between 8 and 25 m/min
  • Scarfing Widths: From 100 mm to 450 mm with selectable patterns
  • Torch Design: Water-cooled torches with customized designs for long reliable service life
  • Ignition System: Instant ignition using economic iron-powder injection
  • Torch Manipulation: Right to left, then left to right for double production capacity from single pass ignition
  • Surface Quality: Minimal ridge height between scarfing passes, scale-free surfaces with universal descaling system
  • Nozzle Options: Round or flat nozzles for excellent surface quality and optimized yield
  • Multiple Programs: Several selectable scarfing programs using the same torch for various width and depth selections
  • Position Flexibility: Slabs can be scarfed in any position, from horizontal to vertical
  • Energy Efficiency: Low fluid and low energy consumption across all operations

Guaranteed Quality & Service Options

The scarfing machines produced by Evertz can be designed, manufactured and installed turnkey for customers wishing to purchase and then operate the facility themselves.

More frequently, we offer scarfing machine installations within the framework of a complete service contract package, which includes our own specialist trained personnel and the day-to-day operation of the activity including all maintenance and repairs. In this way we can guarantee the highest quality, performance and around the clock availability, 24 hours a day and 365 days a year.

The Evertz machine scarfing service is typically offered on a price per square meter or per tonne basis.

The customer is totally relieved of all day-to-day aspects of running the scarfing activity and Evertz is also completely responsible for maintaining a stock of strategic spare parts on site. This solution provides our customers with full service obligations and complete peace of mind.
