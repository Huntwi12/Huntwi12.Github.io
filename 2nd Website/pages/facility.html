Evertz Technology Service USA - Middletown Facility

Evertz Technology Service USA operates from Building 102 at the Midd Cities Industrial Park in Middletown, Ohio. Our strategically located facility provides comprehensive steel grinding and surface conditioning services with access to rail transportation, overhead cranes, and modern industrial infrastructure.

Since establishing our operations in August 2002, we've built a reputation for quality and reliability, processing over 1.6 million tons of steel and specialty alloys. Our facility is equipped with specialized grinding equipment, material handling systems, and a 40-foot reheat furnace capable of temperatures up to 1,000°F.

Midd Cities Industrial Park

The Midd Cities Industrial Park is a premier multi-tenant industrial complex in Butler County, Ohio, offering approximately 600,000 square feet of rentable industrial space across multiple buildings on a 55.79-acre campus.

Park Features

  • Total Site Area: Approximately 55.79 acres
  • Total Building Space: 600,000+ square feet across multiple buildings
  • Property Type: Multi-tenant industrial park
  • Established: Original site development dating to early 1900s, with buildings from 1965+
  • Rail Service: CSX rail access throughout the park
  • Utilities: Gas heating, industrial power, water systems
  • Outdoor Areas: Storage and expansion space available
  • Building Types: Manufacturing, warehouse, storage, and processing facilities

Industrial Neighbors

The Midd Cities Industrial Park is home to a diverse range of industrial and manufacturing companies, creating a collaborative business environment. Notable neighbors include:

  • Avure Technologies: High-pressure processing equipment manufacturer
  • Capital City Group: Equipment rental and crane services
  • HECO – All Systems Go: Motor and powertrain services
  • K & S Industrial Services: Industrial support services
  • Trident Fluid Power: Hydraulic and fluid power systems
  • Standard Labs: Testing and laboratory services

Services Provided From Our Middletown Facility
