Professional Rounds Grinding Services

Evertz Technology Service USA specializes in precision grinding of round steel products, delivering superior surface quality for a wide range of applications. Our rounds grinding capabilities handle diameters from 6 inches to 16 inches with lengths up to 20 feet, ensuring consistent quality and dimensional accuracy.

Whether you need surface conditioning at our Middletown, Ohio facility or on-site grinding services at your location, our experienced team provides the expertise and equipment necessary to meet your exact specifications. We process carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, and specialty alloys with precision and care.

Our rounds grinding services remove surface defects, improve dimensional tolerances, and prepare your products for subsequent processing such as heat treatment, machining, or direct use in manufacturing applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Diameter Range: 6 inches to 16 inches
  • Length Capacity: Up to 20 feet
  • Service Locations: At our facility or on-site at your mill
  • Materials: Carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, specialty alloys
  • Surface Finish: High-quality ground finish per customer specifications
  • Grinding Method: Precision grinding equipment with skilled operators
  • Quality Control: Dimensional inspection and surface quality verification
  • Custom Sizes: Special dimensions available upon request

Our Rounds Grinding Process

1. Material Inspection

Each round product is carefully inspected upon receipt to identify surface defects, dimensional requirements, and specific customer specifications.

2. Surface Preparation

Rounds are cleaned and prepared for grinding operations, ensuring optimal conditions for achieving the desired surface finish.

3. Precision Grinding

Our skilled operators use specialized grinding equipment to remove surface defects while maintaining dimensional accuracy. Grinding parameters are carefully controlled to achieve consistent results across the entire length of each round.

4. Quality Verification

Finished rounds undergo dimensional inspection and surface quality verification to ensure they meet all customer specifications and industry standards.

5. Handling & Logistics

Processed rounds are carefully handled and prepared for shipment via truck or rail, or returned to your production line for on-site services.
