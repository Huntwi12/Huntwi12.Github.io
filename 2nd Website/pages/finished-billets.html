High-Precision Billet Grinding Services

To ensure high surface quality, billet grinding is achieved through precision surface conditioning of square section billets. Evertz Technology Service USA offers comprehensive billet grinding capabilities designed to meet the demanding requirements of steel mills and specialty alloy producers.

Our billet grinding services include adherence to contours, grinding of square cross sections, custom corner profiling to customer request, and meticulous attention to detail regarding quality defects and any surface concerns. We process a wide range of materials including carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, and specialty alloys.

Higher production style machines specific to billet grinding are another one of our specialties. Whether you require facility-based processing at our Middletown location or on-site services at your mill, our experienced team delivers consistent quality and precision.

Square Billet Grinding

Precision Square Billet Processing

Square billets present unique challenges with their corner and edge geometries. Our billet grinding process includes custom corner profiling to meet your specific radius requirements, ensuring proper corner condition for downstream rolling or forging. We maintain precise edge definition while thoroughly conditioning all four faces of the billet.

Custom Corner Profiling

Corner profiling is a critical aspect of square billet grinding. We work to your exact specifications for corner radius, whether you require sharp edges, specific radius profiles, or chamfered corners. This customization ensures your billets are optimally prepared for your specific rolling or forging process.

Surface Conditioning

Our specialized grinding equipment ensures consistent material removal across all four faces of the square billet. We remove surface defects such as seams, cracks, and scale while maintaining dimensional accuracy and proper corner geometry throughout the length of each billet.

Our Billet Grinding Process

1. Material Evaluation

Each billet is assessed for surface condition, defect types, material grade, and cross-sectional geometry. We review your specific requirements for corner profiling and surface finish.

2. Process Planning

Our technical team develops a grinding plan tailored to your billet specifications, including grinding parameters, corner profile requirements, and quality control checkpoints.

3. Precision Grinding

Using specialized billet grinding equipment, our skilled operators systematically condition all surfaces while maintaining geometric accuracy. For square billets, particular attention is paid to corner profiling to achieve your specified radius or edge condition.

4. Surface Inspection

Ground billets are carefully inspected for complete defect removal, surface quality, dimensional accuracy, and proper corner profiling. Any surface concerns are documented and addressed.

5. Quality Documentation

We provide documentation of the grinding process, surface quality verification, and dimensional conformance as required for your quality management system.

6. Material Handling & Transport

Finished billets are handled with care to preserve surface quality and prepared for return to your production line or shipment to the next processing stage.
