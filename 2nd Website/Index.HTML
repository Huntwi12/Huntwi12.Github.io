About Evertz Technology Service USA

Evertz Technology Service USA is a wholly owned subsidiary of The Evertz Group, based in Solingen, Germany. Since establishing our Middletown, Ohio facility in August 2002, we've processed over 1.6 million tons of steel and specialized alloys.

We integrate the know-how of a grinding machine manufacturer with the experience of a professional service provider. Our facility features state-of-the-art equipment, including two overhead cranes (40 and 45 ton capacity) and a 40-foot reheat furnace capable of temperatures up to 1,000°F.
